About the Portal

The STI Parent Portal keeps you connected with your child's academic journey in this free and easy-to-use platform. With the STI Parent Portal, you can now:

  • View your child's grades with just a quick tap of a button
  • Know your child's day-to-day class schedule complete with classroom details and professors
  • Get the latest STI news from all over the STI network
  • Access important academic resources and information
